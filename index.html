The Hidden Beauty of Tulap Falls

Masipi East, Cabagan, Isabela

The clear, reflective main pool surrounded by large rock formations.

Hidden in the quiet barangay of Masipi East, Cabagan, Tulap Falls is a serene natural retreat known for its clear waters, refreshing atmosphere, and untouched beauty. It's one of Cabagan’s true hidden gems—perfect for travelers seeking peace and a genuine connection with nature.

The Tulap Experience

Natural Beauty

Experience a gentle waterfall cascading into a clear, cool pool, surrounded entirely by lush, vibrant greenery.

Scenic Journey

The route passes through picturesque rice fields, groves of coconut trees, and rolling hills, giving you a beautiful preview of the calm beauty ahead.

Relax & Enjoy

The pool and the surrounding rocks are ideal for swimming, having a peaceful picnic, taking stunning photos, or simply relaxing and breathing the fresh air.

Why Visit

Tulap Falls offers a quiet escape from the busy town life—it is simple, unspoiled, and full of natural charm. It’s a favorite weekend spot for families, students, and travelers who deeply appreciate peace, fresh air, and nature’s calm.

Travel Information

Location Barangay Masipi East, Cabagan, Isabela
Map Link View on Google Maps
Best Time to Visit Early morning or on weekends during the dry season for the most pleasant and safest experience.
How to Get There Accessible by local transport; be prepared for short treks to reach the immediate falls area.

Essence of Cabagan’s Countryside

Tulap Falls is a hidden paradise that perfectly captures the quiet, unspoiled beauty of Cabagan’s countryside. With its clear waters, green surroundings, and peaceful ambiance, it serves as a powerful reminder to every visitor that the best destinations are often the most natural and untouched.
